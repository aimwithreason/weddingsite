Murphy Mikulski Wedding Information

Ceremony and Reception will be held at Columbia River Gorge Guesthouse

Timing

The Ceremony will start at 3pm on September 2nd, feel free to arrive anytime after 2pm. Food and merriment to follow.

Directions

Head East on WA Highway 14. Proceed through Camas/ Washougal to the 23 ½ mile marker (7 miles east of Washougal, WA). Turn left on Belle Center Road. The guest house is located on the right, 1.2 miles after you leave Highway 14.

Lodging

There are many options for lodging near Washougal. Most options are in Vancouver, WA which is about 25-30 mins from the guesthouse where the wedding is being celebrated. Downtown Portland is about 40 minutes from the guesthouse.
